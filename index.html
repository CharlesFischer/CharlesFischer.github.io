Things you shouldn't do on social media:

● Don't post personal information such as names, birthdays, current or past residency, or any other current activities such as vacations that display vulnerability

● Do not click on a suspicious email that has an unfamiliar address

● Steer clear of malicious links that would take you to inappropriate or risky web pages

● Make sure you know who you are talking to, especially if it's a friend or tech support

Suspicious email characteristics:

● Link to an unknown or mispelt website

● Unknown sender, check for any spelling differences

● Hyperlinks that lead to an unknown download/website

● Images with embedded code

● Login fields that aren't encrypted; http instead of https

Secure account settings:

● Turn off location services so that the photo you post doesn't carry location data

● Change passwords frequently, use strong passwords

● Turn on alerts for unrecognized logins

● Turn on two-step verification

● Use encryption whenever possible

Strong Passwords:

● Make your passwords long

Example: ridiculouslylongpassword

● Use lots of numbers, incorporate 1337 speak to replace letters with numbers

Example: unb3474b13p455w0rd

● Frequently use symbols

Example: un$beat@able!pass^word

● Don't use personal info such as names in your passwords

Example of what not to do: charlesfischer5

● Capitalize random letters

Example: unBreAkAblEpassWoRd
